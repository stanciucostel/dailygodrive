Privacy Policy – DailyGo Drive

DailyGo Drive respects your privacy and is designed to work without collecting or storing personal data.

Location Data

The app uses your device’s location in real time to provide navigation-related features such as map display, speed camera alerts, and nearby points of interest.
Location data is processed only on your device and is not stored or transmitted to any external servers.

Data Collection

DailyGo Drive does not collect, store, or share personal data.
We do not use analytics services, advertising networks, or third-party tracking tools.

Third-Party Services

Any in-app purchases or subscriptions are handled securely by Apple through the App Store. DailyGo Drive does not have access to your payment information.

Background Usage

Location access may continue in the background to provide driving assistance features such as alerts and notifications, strictly for app functionality.

Children’s Privacy

DailyGo Drive is not specifically directed to children and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated if app functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected in this document.
